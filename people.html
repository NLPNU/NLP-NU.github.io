Dr. Ensaf Mohamed

Assoc. Prof., ITCS, Nile University

Dr. Ensaf H. Mohamed is an associate professor at the School of Information Technology and Computer Science at Nile University. She received her bachelor's degree with honors in computer science, from Helwan University in 2000, followed by a master's degree in computer science, from Helwan in 2006, and a Ph.D. in computer science from Helwan University, Cairo, Egypt, in 2013. She was promoted to associate professor in 2020. Dr. Mohamed is also a researcher in Natural Language Processing (NLP) at the Ministry of Communication and Information Technology (MCIT) from 2020. Her recent research focuses on Natural Language Processing, Text Mining, Social Media Analysis, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, large language models (LLMs), Medical Image Analysis, ontology, and knowledge graphs.

Dr. Ghada Khoriba

Assoc. Prof., ITCS, Nile University

Dr. Ghada Khoriba, an IEEE and ACM member, is an associate professor at the School of Information Technology and Computer Science at Nile University. She received a bachelor's degree from Helwan University in 2000 and an M.Sc. from Helwan University in 2004. She received her Ph.D. in computer science from the University of Tsukuba, Japan, in 2010 and was promoted to associate professor in 2020. She is also an associate professor in the Department of Computer Science at Helwan University, where she has been since 2000. Her research interests include Medical Image Analysis, Machine Learning Techniques and Optimization Problems, Deep Learning Models, Swarm Algorithms, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, LLMs, and Knowledge Graphs
